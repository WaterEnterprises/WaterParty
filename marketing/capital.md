## Part 1: The Geographic Strategy

### Why Recife?

Like Tinder, WaterParty needs **density** to work. A party discovery app is useless with 3 users in São Paulo. We need a critical mass in one city before expanding.

| Factor | Recife Advantage |
|---|---|
| **Population** | 4M+ metro area — large enough for density, small enough to saturate |
| **Nightlife culture** | Rich party scene — beaches, clubs, informal gatherings, forró, funk |
| **University density** | UFPE, UFRPE, UNICAP, Católica — 100K+ students |
| **Tech ecosystem** | Porto Digital — one of Brazil's largest tech parks, startup-friendly |
| **Media market** | One dominant local TV/radio/influencer ecosystem — easier to saturate |
| **BRL currency** | Already fully supported by the currency detection engine |
| **Cost-effective** | Lower CAC than São Paulo or Rio — influencer partnerships are cheaper |

### The Playbook (City-by-City)

```
Phase 1 (Months 1–6): RECIFE — achieve 5K MAU
Phase 2 (Months 6–12): RECIFE at 15K MAU + start SALVADOR
Phase 3 (Months 12–18): RECIFE + SALVADOR + FORTALEZA
Phase 4 (Months 18–24): Expand to SÃO PAULO + RIO
```

**Why this order:** Northeast Brazil has the strongest party culture, lowest CAC, and least competition from US/EU-focused apps. Prove it here first.

### Recife Sponsorship Targets (Local First)

| Sponsor | Type | Why WaterParty |
|---|---|---|
| **Bacalhau / Burburinho / Downtown bars** | Venues | Direct beneficiary — more party-goers = more customers |
| **Galeria Joalina / Marco Zero bars** | Nightlife district | Built-in party district exposure |

| **União de bairros** | Local event organizers | Co-promote block parties and street events |
| **Faculdades (UFPE, UNICAP, UFRPE)** | University | Campus party sponsorships — R$ 2K–5K per event |

### Sponsorship Pitch to AMBEV / Heineken

**Value prop for beverage brands:**
- WaterParty users are **18–35, party-going, beverage consumers**
- Every event created = potential beverage sales
- Brand integration: "Powered by [Brahma/Skol/Heineken]" on tip confirmation, party cards
- Data: see which parties are most popular, when, where
- Exclusive category lockout: only your beer appears in the app

**Suggested pilot:** R$ 15K/month for 3 months in Recife — measure engagement, then expand.

### Sponsorship Pitch to Kwai / TikTok

**Value prop:**
- WaterParty generates **authentic party content** — what TikTok's algorithm rewards
- Users create party videos → share to TikTok → organic cross-promotion
- WaterParty becomes TikTok's "events layer" in Brazil
- Co-marketing: TikTok creators promote WaterParty → WaterParty events generate TikTok content

**Suggested pilot:** Kwai/TikTok provides R$ 30K in ad credits + content creator access. WaterParty integrates "Share to TikTok" and promotes Kwai/TikTok within the app.
