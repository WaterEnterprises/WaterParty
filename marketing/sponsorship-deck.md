## Why Sponsor WaterParty?

WaterParty connects **18–35 year olds** in real-world social settings — the exact demographic brands spend billions trying to reach. Our users are:

- **18–34** (core target: 68% of user base)
- **Urban, social, experience-seeking**
- **High disposable income** — tipping and crowdfunding behaviors prove willingness to spend
- **Hard to reach** — cord-cutters, ad-blockers, TikTok-native

Every sponsorship dollar puts your brand directly in front of users **while they're in a party-going mindset** — open, social, and receptive.
