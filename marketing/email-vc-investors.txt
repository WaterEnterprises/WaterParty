Subject: WaterParty — Reinventing Nightlife Discovery for a $150B Global Market

Dear Partner,

I am writing to introduce you to WaterParty, a venture I am building under Water Enterprises / Stellarium Foundation. We are a technology company addressing a fundamental gap in one of the largest yet most fragmented consumer markets in the world: nightlife and social discovery.

The Problem

Today, a young adult planning a night out must juggle between 4–6 different applications: Instagram to find out where people are going, WhatsApp to coordinate with friends, Uber or 99 to get there, Venmo/Pix to split bills or pay cover charges, and sometimes a separate ticketing platform. There is no single, dedicated layer for discovering real-world social experiences. This fragmentation costs consumers time and money, and it costs venues and event organizers billions in lost revenue from inefficient discovery and last-minute cancellations.

The global nightlife and events industry is estimated at over $150 billion annually. Yet it remains one of the few large consumer verticals without a dominant digital platform.

The Solution: WaterParty

WaterParty is a Tinder-style discovery application that unifies the entire party-going journey into one fluid experience:

• Swipe to discover parties, events, and social gatherings nearby
• RSVP and see which friends are attending
• Real-time chat with hosts and other attendees
• Integrated payments through Stripe Connect — tipping, crowdfunding for events, and cover charges, all in-app
• Automatic currency detection via GPS — seamless cross-border functionality
• Reputation system for hosts and attendees — building trust in the community

We are not building a feature. We are building the operating system for nightlife.

Traction & Technology

We have a fully functional, production-ready MVP built on modern infrastructure:

• Front-end: React 19 + TypeScript + Vite
• Back-end: Bun runtime + Turso (edge SQLite)
• Payments: Stripe Connect (tipping + crowdfunding via Stripe)
• Real-time: WebSockets for chat and live notifications
• Cross-platform: iOS + Android via Capacitor
• GPS-based currency detection working and tested
• Active deployment with real users

Market Opportunity

We are launching our first market in Recife, Brazil — a city of 4 million people with over 100,000 university students across UFPE, UFRPE, and UNICAP. Recife is the perfect density play: a concentrated urban population with a vibrant nightlife culture and a large young adult demographic.

From Recife, we expand to the 100+ cities across Latin America with similar density profiles, then to the global market. Our GPS-based currency detection makes international expansion technically seamless from day one.

Why Now?

Three macro trends converge in our favor:
1. Post-pandemic pent-up demand for real-world social experiences is at an all-time high
2. Gen Z and young Millennials are increasingly rejecting algorithm-driven social media in favor of real-world connection
3. The creator economy has trained a generation to monetize their passions — WaterParty enables anyone to become a party host and earn through our platform
