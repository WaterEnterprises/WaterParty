## The Opportunity

The global nightlife and social events market exceeds **$150B annually**. Yet the tools for discovering spontaneous, real-world social gatherings remain fragmented and outdated. Facebook Events is bloated, Meetup is rigid, and no existing platform combines real-time discovery with social profiles, messaging, and seamless money movement.

18–35 year olds — the most social, smartphone-native generation — are underserved by existing solutions. They want to discover parties, connect with people, and participate in event economies **without leaving one app**.

## The Solution

WaterParty is a cross-platform mobile app that lets users:

- **Discover** — Swipe through nearby parties Tinder-style
- **Connect** — Chat with attendees and hosts in real-time
- **Host** — Create parties with location, time, guest limits, and vibes
- **Participate** — Send tips, contribute to crowdfunded events, and earn trust scores
- **Go Global** — Multi-currency support auto-detected from GPS coordinates

The app is **fully functional in production** — not a prototype, not a mockup.

## Key Differentiators

| Advantage | Why It Matters |
|---|---|
| **Swipe-first discovery** | Familiar, addictive UX — low learning curve, high engagement |
| **Integrated payments** | Tips and crowdfunding handled in-app via Stripe — no Venmo splits |
| **Auto-currency detection** | GPS-based currency switching works seamlessly across borders |
| **Real-time everything** | WebSocket-based chat and feed updates — no pull-to-refresh needed |
| **Rich social profiles** | Instagram, X, Telegram, WhatsApp, Facebook links + trust scores + verifiable hosting history |
| **Cross-platform from day one** | iOS + Android via Capacitor — single React codebase |
