## What is FACEPE?

**FACEPE** (Fundação de Amparo à Ciência e Tecnologia do Estado de Pernambuco) is Pernambuco's state research and innovation funding foundation. They offer **non-dilutive grants** (subvenção econômica — you don't give up equity) for tech startups, research, and innovation projects based in Pernambuco.

**Website:** [facepe.br](https://www.facepe.br/)  
**Submissions platform:** [agil.facepe.br](https://agil.facepe.br/)  
**Instagram:** [@facepe_oficial](https://www.instagram.com/facepe_oficial/) (first announcements go here)

### 2. TECNOVA (FINEP + FACEPE) — Larger Grants

| Detail | Info |
|---|---|
| **What** | R&D subvenção for micro/small enterprises to develop innovative products |
| **Amount** | R$200K–R$500K+ (varies by edital) |
| **Stage** | More mature than Centelha — needs existing product + revenue |
| **Eligibility** | ME/EPP with CNPJ — may need to show revenue |
| **Status** | **CLOSED** — no current open call |
| **Why WP fits** | Would fund GPS currency detection engine improvements, AI recommendation scaling |

|---|---|

## 💡 Pro Tip

FACEPE editals reward **technological innovation**, not just business model innovation. When applying, emphasize:

- **GPS-based automatic currency detection** (your unique tech)
- **Real-time WebSocket event sync** (technical complexity)
- **AI-powered recommendation engine** (future scope)
- **Cross-platform React Native / Capacitor** (modern stack)

This scores higher than "we're a party app" — FACEPE wants to fund tech R&D, not marketing.
