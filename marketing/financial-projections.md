## 1. Revenue Model Overview

WaterParty generates revenue through **four streams**, with two live at launch and two on the roadmap.

| Stream | Status | Mechanics | Est. Margin |
|---|---|---|---|
| **Tips Processing Fee** | ✅ Live | 5% fee on every monetary tip sent via Stripe PaymentIntents | 70–80% (after Stripe fees) |
| **Crowdfunding Platform Fee** | ✅ Live | 5% fee on party crowdfunding campaigns | 70–80% (after Stripe fees) |
| **AdMob Advertising** | ✅ Live | Interstitial + rewarded video ads | 60–70% (Google rev share) |
| **Promoted Parties** | 🔜 Q2 2026 | CPM-based paid placement in swipe queue | 85–90% |
| **Power Host Subscription** | 🔜 Q3 2026 | $9.99/mo — analytics, promo tools, priority support | 90%+ |

## 2. Unit Economics

### Assumptions (Year 1 targets)

| Metric | Estimate | Benchmark |
|---|---|---|
| **CAC (User Acquisition Cost)** | $2.50–$4.00 | Organic + referral + paid social |
| **CAC (paid campaigns)** | $5.00–$8.00 | Instagram/TikTok ads |
| **Average Revenue Per User (ARPU) / mo** | $0.80–$1.50 | Tipping + ads + crowdfunding fees |
| **ARPU (paying users)** | $4.00–$7.00 | Users who send tips or host events |
| **LTV (12 months, avg user)** | $9.60–$18.00 | ARPU × 12 |
| **LTV (12 months, paying user)** | $48.00–$84.00 | Paying ARPU × 12 |
| **Payback Period (paid acquisition)** | 3–6 months | CAC ÷ monthly ARPU |
| **Tip Conversion Rate** | 8–15% | % of users who send a tip |
| **Host Conversion Rate** | 3–7% | % of users who create a party |
| **Crowdfunding Participation** | 2–5% | % of users who contribute to a campaign |
| **Monthly Churn** | 15–25% | Standard for social apps |
| **DAU/MAU Ratio** | 25–35% | Healthy for social discovery |

### Unit Economics at Scale (Year 3, 500K MAU)

| Metric | Conservative | Base | Aggressive |
|---|---|---|---|
| **MAU** | 300K | 500K | 1M |
| **ARPU / mo** | $0.80 | $1.20 | $1.80 |
| **Monthly Revenue** | $240K | $600K | $1.8M |
| **CAC (blended)** | $3.00 | $2.50 | $2.00 |
| **LTV / CAC Ratio** | 3.2× | 5.8× | 10.8× |

## 4. Key Drivers of Growth

### User Acquisition Channels (Projected Cost per User)

| Channel | Cost per Install | Volume Potential | Notes |
|---|---|---|---|
| Organic (ASO, referrals, word-of-mouth) | $0.00 | Moderate | High-quality users, lower churn |
| Campus ambassadors | $1.00–$2.00 | High | Targeted 18–22 demographic |
| TikTok/Instagram content | $0.50–$1.50 | Very High | Viral potential |
| Influencer partnerships | $2.00–$5.00 | Moderate | Brand credibility |
| Paid social ads | $5.00–$8.00 | Very High | Scalable but expensive |

### Network Effects

Each host brings **15–50 attendees** on average. Hosts are the highest-LTV users (they tip, crowdfund, and create recurring events). Investing in host acquisition creates asymmetric returns.
