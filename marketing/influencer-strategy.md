## 🎯 Why Influencers Matter for WaterParty

- Nightlife and events are **visually driven** — perfect for Instagram Stories, TikTok Reels, and YouTube Shorts
- WaterParty needs **density** to work — influencers can kickstart the network effect in Recife
- Young adults (18–35) trust influencer recommendations over traditional ads
- **Media for Equity** models let you partner without cash upfront

## 🤝 Media for Equity — Influencers as Investors

### What Is It?
Influencers promote your app in exchange for **equity** instead of cash. They become stakeholders in your success.

### Typical Deal Structure

| Component | Typical Terms |
|---|---|
| **Equity** | 0.1% – 2% of company |
| **Vesting** | 2–4 year schedule (earned gradually) |
| **Cliff** | 6–12 month cliff |
| **KPIs** | Measurable deliverables (installs, engagement, posts) |
| **Exclusivity** | Cannot promote competing apps |
| **Content commitment** | X posts/stories per month |

### Agencies That Broker Media for Equity Deals

## 🎯 Recommended Outreach Targets (Recife)

### Top 5 Micro-Influencer Profiles to Find

1. **Party/event promoters** — already have an audience that goes out in Recife
2. **University student creators** (UFPE, UFRPE, UNICAP) — 100K+ students in Recife
3. **Lifestyle/vloggers** — document nightlife, restaurants, culture
4. **Music/dance creators** — aligned with party culture
5. **Local comedians** — high engagement, strong local following

### Outreach Script (Portuguese)

> *"Oi [Nome]! Sou John, founder do WaterParty — um app tipo Tinder pra descobrir festas em Recife. Estamos lançando na cidade e adorei seu conteúdo! Queria te convidar pra testar o app e conversar sobre uma parceria. Podemos fazer um teste com uns stories e, se rolar química, pensar em algo mais longo — inclusive participação na empresa. Topa?"*

### Outreach Script (English)

Would love to have you try the app and chat about a partnership. We could start with some stories and, if there's chemistry, explore a longer-term deal — including equity. Interested?"*
